This is what inspires the team at UTrim

UTrim is a free and easy-to-use tool that allows you to shorten long URLs. With UTrim, you can create short, memorable URLs that are easy to share.

UTrim is perfect for:
- Sharing links on social media
- Sending links in emails
- Including links in your website or blog
- Sharing links through QRCodes

UTrim is also a great way to track clicks on your links. With UTrim, you can see how many people have clicked on your links and where they are coming from.
To use Red Route, simply enter the long URL in the box and click on the "Shorten" button. it will then generate a short, unique URL for you. You can then copy and paste the short URL wherever you want to share it.
